The Houston Chronicle’s Primary Voters Guide

We invited candidates in Houston’s contested primary races to answer questions on issues facing the state and how they plan to transform Texas. The candidate’s responses are unedited and presented as submitted to the Houston Chronicle. Some of the candidates, including Gov. Greg Abbott, did not respond to our questionnaire.
